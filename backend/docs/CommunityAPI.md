## Endpoints Summary
- `GET /api/v1/community/feed`: Fetch paginated community feed dispatches.
- `POST /api/v1/community/posts`: Create a new photo, story, journal, or review post.
- `GET /api/v1/community/profiles/:userId`: Fetch public traveler profile, badges, and stats.
- `GET /api/v1/community/groups`: Fetch active travel groups.
- `GET /api/v1/community/recommendations`: AI recommendations for travelers to follow.
