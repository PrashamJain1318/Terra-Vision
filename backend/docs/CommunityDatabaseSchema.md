- `CommunityPost`: Feed posts (photo, story, journal, review, checkin).
- `CommunityJournal`: Long-form travel blogs and itineraries.
- `CommunityGroup`: Niche traveler communities.
- `CommunityReview`: Ratings & reviews for spots and routes.
- `CommunityFollow`: Social graph follow linkages.
